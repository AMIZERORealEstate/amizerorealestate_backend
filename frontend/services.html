Our Services

Comprehensive real estate solutions tailored to your needs

Property & Asset Management

We handle comprehensive property management services including rent collection, tenant management, lease administration, and maintenance coordination. Our facility management covers security, cleaning, landscaping, utilities, and vacancy management to maximize your property value.

Building Permit & Compliance

We assist in obtaining building permits and ensuring legal compliance for your projects. From documentation to approvals, we simplify the process, saving you time and preventing delays.

Property Valuation

We provide detailed, market-based valuation reports to guide smart investments and ensure proper pricing for buying or selling property.

Real Estate Brokerage

We connect buyers and sellers, ensuring smooth real estate transactions. Our expert guidance, market knowledge, and negotiation skills help you secure the best deals efficiently.

Quantity Survey

We provide cost estimation, budgeting, and financial planning for construction projects. Our experts ensure cost-effective procurement and accurate material assessment.

Development & Construction Support

We offer complete construction support from project feasibility studies and land acquisition to construction project management. Our services include architecture and design coordination, interior design, and fit-outs to bring your vision to reality.

Financial & Investment Services

We provide expert real estate investment advisory and property portfolio management services. Our comprehensive financial support includes mortgage and financing assistance, property tax advisory, compliance guidance, and insurance brokerage including property and rental guarantee insurance.

Market & Research

We deliver in-depth real estate market analysis and investment risk assessments to inform your decisions. Our research services include demographic and location studies, rental price benchmarking, and comprehensive market intelligence to give you competitive advantages.

Legal & Advisory

We provide comprehensive legal support for real estate transactions including contract drafting and review. Our advisory services cover mediation in property disputes, estate planning, and inheritance property handling to ensure smooth and compliant transactions.
